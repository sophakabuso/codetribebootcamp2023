Our Story

It all started in 1982 when I got to know how advanced Technology is around the world when it comes to Agriculture, Manufacturing, Mining, Education, etc. Certain countries experience extreme bad weather conditions for more or less than a year but still manage to produce enough food for the nation and for export as well. Coming from Africa where Technology is not fully utilised to empower people economically and otherwise, MANEX(PTY LTD) was established specifically to facilitate the use of advancing technologies in various spheres of our economy. MANEX(PTY LTD) considers itself as a bridge that connects Investors and the Market for sustainable development and economic growth. We believe in inclusive and sustainable industries to bring about prosperity.
